An inhospitable ice ball orbiting a dim star, Soul tests its inhabitants’ survival abilities every day. Lacking axial tilt in its circular orbit, there are no significant seasonal changes; just long days of bright but cold sunlight and long nights of biting cold. An equator‐girdling network of immense greenhouses, using fusion power for heat and sunlamps, grow food for the locals. The capital city is located on the perpetual terminator at the planet’s northern pole.
