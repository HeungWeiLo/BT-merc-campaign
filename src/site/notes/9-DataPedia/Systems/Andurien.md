Andurien is the Capital of the Duchy of Andurien and as such suffered greatly for the Andurien Succession of the 3030s. While the planet and its infrastructure returned to previous levels, the planet was slow to recover the financial prestige it once possessed. The planet has significant industrial facilities producing a wealth of civilian and military goods. However, after the Andurien Succession, all military manufacturing was turned over to the Free Worlds League instead of being controlled by the Duchy of Andurien.
